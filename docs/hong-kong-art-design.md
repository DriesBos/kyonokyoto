## Held back

- **Current Plans** — strong experimental programme, but official site currently shows no events and no clear visitor address.
- **Cattle Depot Artist Village** — venue container; 1a space and Videotage provide concrete feeds.
- **HKDI Material Resource Centre** and **matériO Hong Kong** — resource libraries, not recurring public exhibition feeds.
- **HART Haus**, **DX design hub**, **SC Gallery**, **Antenna Space**, **WURE AREA**, and **To Art House** — official sites were not reliably fetchable during source review.
- **Gate33 Gallery at AIRSIDE**, **Axel Vervoordt Gallery Hong Kong**, and **Flowers Gallery Hong Kong** — listed official URLs currently return 404.
- **Sotheby's Maison** and **Opera Gallery Hong Kong** — no stable Hong Kong-scoped exhibition feed found.
- **PHD Group**, **Thy Lab**, and **Square Street Gallery** — no crawlable current exhibition listing found on official site.
- **City Gallery** — homepage exposes dated archive pages, but proposed current-exhibitions route returns 404.
- **Tai Kwun** — current `robots.txt` disallows all crawling for general user agents, including the official programme route.
- Existing purple markers and previously visited locations — excluded to keep this an expansion list.
